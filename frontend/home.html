Features

Why NagarSeva?

A complete system designed for citizens, administrators, and workers.

🤖
Gemini AI Classification
Google’s free Gemini AI analyzes every complaint and automatically determines its priority—whether it’s a health hazard, flooding, or a minor issue.
📷
Photo Upload Support
Attach up to 5 photos of the problem—clearer evidence ensures faster resolution.
📊
Real-time Dashboard
The admin can view all complaints in one place—sorted with filters for status, priority, and category.
👷
Worker Assignment
The admin directly assigns tasks to the field worker. The worker sees their tasks sorted by AI urgency score.
🔔
Status Tracking
Citizens can track the status of their complaints—at every step from Pending to Resolved.
🔐
Secure JWT Login
Separate secure portals for citizens, administrators, and workers with JWT token authentication.
